First 8 counts

Each student will start facing their partner -- Link hands one over the other. On “Hot” the person on the right (facing the audience) drops (on count 5) and pops back up (on count 6). Then on “Dense” the other partner repeats the same motion but on counts 7 & 8.

2nd 8 counts

Then on beat one spin with your partner in a circle for 7 counts then on the 8th count jump out and face the audience.

3rd 8 counts

Both partners lean left for 2 counts then right for 2 counts. (pretending to be cold) Person facing away from the audience moves to the right, and the person in the audience blows wind at them. Drop down on knees, hold hands, then swing outside arm in and hold both hands again.

4th 8 counts

For 2 counts the person on the right (building a wall) stand up for 2 counts face each other and point hand to make a pyramid, Partners will slide inward with inward arm 45 degrees down and outside arm 45 degree up

Last 8 counts

At “unraveling the mystery” partner in the back spins partner in the front for 2 counts The left person spins the right person for 2 counts Drop shoulder towards your partner for 4 counts while also moving to a line position. The person in the front creates a little star, then the person in the back makes a bigger star 1 beat after
